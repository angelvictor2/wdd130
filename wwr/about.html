Mazate Rafting

Our Purpose: To ignite an adventurous spirit in every individual, fostering a deep connection with nature and creating unforgettable memories on the river. Our Mission: To provide premier white water rafting experiences that are safe, exhilarating, and educational. Our Creed: We believe in the power of the river. It is our classroom, our playground, and our guide.

History

Initially Mazatenango, Suchitepéquez, Guatemala; later expanding to include a satellite office closer to river launch points.The Seed of an Idea (Early 2000s) The story of Mazate Rafting begins not with a roar, but with a quiet observation. Alejandro Ramirez, a young, adventurous spirit from Mazatenango, Suchitepéquez, had always been drawn to the rivers that snaked through the lush, volcanic landscape of Guatemala. While working in the burgeoning ecotourism sector, primarily as a guide for hiking and birdwatching tours, he noticed a gap.
