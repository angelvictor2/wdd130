Our Top Rafting Trips

Canyon Rapids Adventure

Experience the thrill of conquering Class III and IV rapids as you wind through narrow canyons with breathtaking views. Perfect for adrenaline seekers looking for a full-day challenge surrounded by towering cliffs and rushing waters.

Duration: 6 hours | Difficulty: Intermediate to Advanced

Family River Ride

This gentle rafting trip is designed for families and beginners. Float along calm waters with just a splash of excitement while enjoying stunning nature, wildlife sightings, and plenty of time for photos and fun along the riverbank.

Duration: 2.5 hours | Difficulty: Easy

Sunset Rafting Retreat

Wind down the day with a relaxing rafting journey timed perfectly to catch the golden sunset. This scenic float is ideal for couples, groups of friends, or anyone who wants a peaceful way to connect with nature.

Duration: 3 hours | Difficulty: Easy to Moderate
